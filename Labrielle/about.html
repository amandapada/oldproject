About Us

La Brielle Gems and Wears is a well-established fashion shop situated on the beautiful Isle of Barnawa, Kaduna with an online presence. We are perfectly located for local and international customers to find their perfect dress. We stock an exquisite collection of dresses and gems from some of the best and most recognisable designers around.

We are proud of our excellent reputation and outstanding customer service. You and your friends and well-wishers party will be the centre of attention, with our dedicated consultants devoting their time to you.
